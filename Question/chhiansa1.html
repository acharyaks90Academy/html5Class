About Chhainsa

According to Census 2011 information the location code or village code of Chhainsa village is 063562. Chhainsa village is located in Ballabgarh Tehsil of Faridabad district in Haryana, India. It is situated 17km away from sub-district headquarter Ballabgarh and 25km away from district headquarter Faridabad. As per 2009 stats, Chhainsa village is also a gram panchayat.

The total geographical area of village is 2174 hectares. Chhainsa has a total population of 14,216 peoples. There are about 2,483 houses in Chhainsa village. As per 2019 stats, Chhainsa villages comes under Prithla assembly & Faridabad parliamentary constituency. Ballabgarh is nearest town to Chhainsa.
